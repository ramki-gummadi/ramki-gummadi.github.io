Ramki Gummadi

 

I am currently a senior researcher at Vicarious, an AI company in the SF Bay Area. Prior to this between 2014-2016, I worked as a data scientist & software engineer at Facebook in the ads delivery group and in the marketplace ranking group. Before that, I was then a postdoc at Stanford until spring 2014, working on multiarmed bandits and mechanism design in Prof. Ramesh Johari's group, where I also collaborated with Prof. Mohsen Bayati in the GSB. From Spring to Fall of 2014, I was a postdoc at the University of Alberta in the RLAI Lab, hosted by Prof. Dale Schuurmans. I received my Ph.D. from the department of ECE at the University of Illinois in Urbana-Champaign in December 2011 with a dissertation on rateless erasure codes for communication networks and MDP's for broadcast scheduling. At UIUC, I was part of the Decision and Control Lab in the CSL and was advised by Prof. R.S. Sreenivas. During my Ph.D., I also had the pleasure of collaborating with Prof. Amin Shokrollahi, Dr. Laurent Massoulie, Prof. Alexandre Proutiere and Dr. Peter Key.
