Ramki Gummadi

 

Welcome to my research homepage. Please email me at gsrk84@gmail.com if you are interested in the latest copy of a draft listed here that is not available for download.

Bio: I got my Ph.D. in EE from the U of Illinois in December 2011 with a dissertation on rateless erasure codes for communication networks. I was then a postdoc at Stanford until spring 2014, working on multiarmed bandits and mechanism design. I spent summer 2014 working on topics in representation learning in the RLAI lab at U of Alberta. I am now employed by Facebook as a data scientist, where I currently work on the backend ad auctions delivery/pacing systems.

Interests: AI, statistical inference, information theory & rateless erasure codes, mechanism design and dynamic auctions.
